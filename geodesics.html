The Characterization Theorem

Theorem 1 (Characterization of Geodesics). Let \(I = [a, b] \subset \mathbb{R}^{}\) be a compact interval and let \(\gamma : I \rightarrow M\) be a smooth curve. Then the following are equivalent.
